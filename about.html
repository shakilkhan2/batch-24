About Batch 24 Alumni

We are a group of passionate individuals who graduated together in 2019 from the Government Teachers' Training College, Dhaka. Our journey over the years has been filled with learning, growth, and countless memories. Now, we are here to celebrate our achievements and stay connected as we move forward in our careers and lives.

The Batch 24 Alumni page is a place for us to reconnect, share memories, and support one another. Whether you're looking to network, share stories, or just catch up with old friends, this site is for you!
